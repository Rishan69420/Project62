Minecraft

Minecraft is a fun, engaging video game. You can either play by yourself, with a friend, or with random people across the world! The game looks just like a boring block game, but you can optimize how you want to play. If you want to play a fun minigame, you can join a server. If you want to play survival mode, then create a world. If you want to build anything; from a dirt shack, to a huge castle, then go into creative mode! There are many ways to play Minecraft, and installing mods or modpacks, you can alter your gameplay.
